We've reached 1 Million!

After more than two decades serving older people and visually challenged people across the country, the ITN Nationwide Network gave its 1 millionth ride this month, May 2018. A million errands run, friends visited, appointments kept. It's a remarkable milestone, the result of dedicated work from thousands of community members nationwide. We offer a million thanks to our members, donors, drivers, board members, volunteers and staff. Every one of them is a part of ITNâ€™s success.

In recognition of this remarkable achievement, the U.S. Senate recognized ITNAmerica for our years of work supporting lifelong mobility. Senators Susan Collins and Angus King came to our offices in Westbrook, Maine, to present ITN founder Katherine Freund with an official copy of the resolution honoring the organization she has spent decades building. One million rides, and it is only a start.

It is good to dream big. It is even better to make it happen.
