Become an ITN Affiliate

ITN brings sustainable, dignified transportation to communities across the country. We partner with local individuals or organizations interested in using our time-tested, supported model to their community. With ITNAmerica’s guidance and expertise, a dedicated group can build a nonprofit senior transportation system anywhere. And by plugging into the ITN network, the benefits of membership — ride credits, CarTrade, Healthy Miles, Road Scholarships, Personal Transportation Accounts, and more — are magnified. ITNCountry Communities

ITNCountry is now working with a handful of specially selected communities to pilot test a new on-line learning system for small and rural communities. These are partner cities and towns that struggle to support sustainable community transportation services. But through ITNCountry, they gain access to the same model programs that work in larger, higher density locations. This is the first step to launching ITNCountry nationally.
