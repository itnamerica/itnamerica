10,000 Americans turn 70 every day.

Life. It’s about going places — shopping, visiting friends, trips to the doctor — but as we get older going places gets harder. Everything from walking up stairs to operating an automobile grows more difficult with age. We might turn to friends, neighbors and family members for help, but once we make the decision to stop driving it’s easy to find ourselves stranded. Life outside the driver’s seat can carry a heavy toll.

But no one should be trapped because they stop driving. ITNAmerica is dedicated to senior mobility. We ensure outliving the ability to drive safely doesn’t lead to a life of isolation. Because life is about going places, at any age.

Regeneron and ITNAmerica

ITNAmerica is proud to recognize Regeneron Pharmaceuticals, Inc. as a corporate sponsor committed to helping raise awareness of the challenges associated with vision impairment, low vision, senior mobility issues and the shortage of locally-available transportation options for seniors. As the National Pharmaceutical Eye-care Services Sponsor for ITNAmerica and our affiliate communities, Regeneron Pharmaceuticals is proud to support ITN's mission to promote safe senior mobility.
